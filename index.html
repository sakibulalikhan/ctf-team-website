About Us

HiddenInvestigations takes pride in being a forefront Capture The Flag (CTF) team in Bangladesh, maintaining a consistent ranking among the nation's elite. Our impressive track record in international and national CTF competitions reflects our team's unwavering passion for cybersecurity, dedication to problem-solving, and commitment to pushing the boundaries of digital security.

Our mission is to delve into the core of cybersecurity, unveiling hidden vulnerabilities and mastering the art of solving intricate challenges. We thrive on pushing the boundaries of knowledge, fostering an environment where continuous skill refinement is paramount, and embracing the exhilarating pursuit of uncovering cyber threats in the digital realm.

HiddenInvestigations envisions a future where cybersecurity is a shared responsibility, and the foundational skills to defend against digital threats are widespread. We aspire to be torchbearers for the next generation of cybersecurity professionals, urging them to explore the dynamic and challenging world of CTFs. Through leading by example and consistently pushing our limits, we aim to contribute significantly to enhancing the overall resilience of the digital landscape.
